So how does it all stack up? Now that we know everything about the Average Joe Noder, it is perhaps interesting to see how the Not-So Average Joe Noder does... In other words, we're going to look at the top end of the bell curve; the people with a lot of writeups, and those that consistently score high reputations for those.

The first ranking I put up here is a ranking by highest Median Node-Fu. The following ranking consists of noders that have 150 writeups or more (the Level 4 requirement) and consistently write high-reputation writeups (a Median Node-Fu=10 means that 50% of a noder's writeups have reputation=10 or more).
